# LogiFlow Operations Platform

## Subtitle

A logistics operations dashboard for shipment booking, parcel tracking, client visibility, and delivery workflow management.

## Problem

Growing logistics teams often manage shipments, clients, payments, and delivery updates across spreadsheets, phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and disconnected tools. This creates poor visibility, delayed updates, missed follow-ups, and manual operational overhead.

## Solution

LogiFlow centralizes logistics operations into one platform with shipment booking, public tracking, client records, delivery status workflows, driver assignment, payment visibility, and operational dashboards.

## Key Features

- Shipment booking and tracking
- Admin operations dashboard
- Client and driver management
- Delivery status workflow
- Payment and invoice tracking
- Public parcel tracking page
- Support issue tracking
- Role-based platform structure

## Business Value

- Better shipment visibility
- Reduced manual tracking
- Cleaner client communication
- Faster status updates
- Centralized operations
- Scalable logistics workflow foundation
